About Cloud Film

Cloud Film Ltd produces online interactive films addressing the issues surrounding personal, medical and emotional issues. These problems are faced not only by individuals, but often their families and significant others.

Our intention is to provide comprehensive support for people in these situations through interactive film, which includes dramatisation, interviews with relevant experts, ‘How To’ sections, and links to external support networks.

“ABOUT JACK” is our first project which will support the families of children with Life-Limiting Conditions. Once the project is complete it will be available online and free for anyone who needs to use it.

Future projects under consideration include the following:

Mental Health Support including:

PTSD
Eating Disorders
Depression and anxiety
Living well with Dementia
Domestic Abuse
Living with a Terminal Illness
Coping with Bereavement

Our approach can also be used successfully within business environments to address relevant issues.
